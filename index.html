About Me

A Creative at Heart

As a recent graduate with a degree in art history and theory, I wasn't sure what I wanted to do with my career.

But then I realized: what better way to merge my passions for writing and visual communication than through web development? That is all this webpage is. It is written content in a visually compelling format.

Through the Thinkful™ Software Engineering Program, I'm learning several skills to get me there:

  1. HTML & CSS
  2. Javascript and its frameworks
  3. Backend Development
  4. Collaboration through GitHub

The program has also given me the opportunity to see some of the amazing work of other programmers. I am excited to see what others keep creating and to get to work with more creative minds out there.

I will be posting updates on projects here, so keep checking in.
